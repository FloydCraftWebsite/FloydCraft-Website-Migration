  We've added the FarLands to our EpicWorld
 
The FarLands are a unique terrain that came the early beta releases of Minecraft, the FarLands would generate when walking out over 12000 chunks, they were too glitchy to actually build in but they were fun to explore. We ReCreated the FarLands at 6000 chunks out, they are an almost perfect copy and are safe to build and play survival in, You'll likely find some strange things out there and face a lot of mobs so make sure you have a strong set of armor and a good weapon, There is no warp, if you want to go you must adventure out.
