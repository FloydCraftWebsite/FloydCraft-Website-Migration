Creative Minecraft Solutions.

We use Minecraft to create experiences, communities, and learning environments.

 

 

 

Blockworks is a collective of over 60 designers, animators, artists and developers from around the world.

 

Gaming

We work with some of the world's largest YouTubers, servers and gaming networks to design refreshing content and revitalize both individual worlds and server experiences.

media

Using Minecraft as a digital arts platform, we work with museums, galleries, corporations and film studios to produce a variety of creative projects; ranging from films and animations, to art installations and interactive exhibits.

education

Minecraft is a language that young people understand. We create functional, engaging and effective learning environments in Minecraft for museums and galleries from across the world.
