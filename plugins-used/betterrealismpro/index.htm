BetterRealismPro [Removed]

May 16, 2019June 3, 2020rmellis Leave a comment
Source: https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/better-realism-pro-makes-mc-more-realistic-great-for-rpg-survival.41599/
​


BetterRealism Pro is a plugin which changes things up to help make MC feel more realistic, something any server could do with.

I actively take suggestions, if you want something added just let me know. I’m always looking for things to add. (Assuming they enhance realism)

Support Discord HERE
​

Craftable Diving Helmets
Extremely Configurable, offers bonuses when used to mine clay from the ocean floor and provides the user with night vision as well as water breathing. (Configurable name + lore!)

Realistic Spiders
Offers you the ability to have spiders shoot webs at players which decay over time.

Rideable ePearls
Lets you ride your enderpearls! EXAMPLE 1

Realistic Squids
Squids have a 1 in 75 chance of blinding a player! EXAMPLE 1

World Management
Disable this plugin on particular worlds if you want.

Realistic Drops
Provides you with realistic loot tables for example villagers can drop emeralds and chickens can drop eggs on death.

Decaying Leaves
Can have a chance of dropping sticks and bird eggs! EXAMPLE 1 EXAMPLE 2

Trails
Stamping grass into dirt, options to limit to a permission. EXMAPLE 1

Language Support
All messages are configurable

Blood Effects
From taking damage from living entities (includes players) Example 1

Death Bones
Extremely configurable, offers you the choice of having custom items dropped on death. Example 1Example 2 Example 3

Craftable bandages
Custom recipe for crafting bandages which offer you 6 seconds of regeneration per use.

Ore deposits
Random chances of getting ores/flint from stone below a Ylvl of your choosing.

Rotten Flesh to leather furnace recipe
Gives you the option to smelt rotten flesh into leather using a normal furnace.

Custom Realistic Recipes





1 paper + 1 ink (not 64)





Negative Hunger
Negative potion effects when hungry

Starvation
Slowness if your hunger bar is empty

Furnaces
Furnaces will store heat after a successful smelt making future smelting faster.

throwableTNT
Allow your players to instantly throw tnt by left clicking, currently doesn’t support 1.8 but can do if requested.

Frost Bite
Negative potion effects after taking drowning damage in cold biomes, extremely configurable.

Sugar Rush
Speed effects after eating sugary food taken from config

Realistic sounds
Configurable sound effects on particular events.

Dragon Drops
Allows you to make dragons drop their skulls & eggs, great for multiplayer servers which allow the end.

Mobs Drop Bones
Allows you to choose which mobs should drop bones. Example 1

Tall Grass Seeds
Allows you to have a 100% drop rate on seeds when breaking tall grass with any kind of hoe.
